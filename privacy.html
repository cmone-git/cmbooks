Introduction

At CM Filings and CM TaxHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our mobile application and related tax filing services.

By accessing or using CM TaxHub, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Information We Collect

To provide accurate tax preparation and filing services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, Date of Birth, PAN, Aadhaar number, and contact details (email, phone number).
  • Financial Data: Bank account statements, Form 16, income details, investment proofs, and trading statements (VDA/US Stocks).
  • App Usage Data: Device information, IP addresses, and crash reports to improve app stability.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used for the provision of our services, including:

  • Preparing and filing your Income Tax Returns (ITR) with the Government Tax Portal.
  • Communicating with you regarding tax compliance deadlines, discrepancies, or required actions.
  • Processing secure payments for our services or advance tax dues.
  • Improving our application interface and user experience.

Data Security & Encryption

We implement bank-grade security protocols to protect your sensitive data. All data transmitted between your device and our servers (including file uploads) is encrypted using SSL/TLS encryption.

Your financial documents and passwords are never shared with unauthorized personnel. Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized tax professionals working directly on your file.
